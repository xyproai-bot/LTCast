## Press license

Reviewers and trade-press journalists can request a one-year Pro license for coverage purposes. Terms:

- Not transferable.
- Revoked if the coverage is undisclosed paid placement that pretends otherwise.
- Issued within 24h of a verifiable press request (outlet name, writer byline, publication URL).

## Embargo policy

We will honour an embargo if:

1. You email in advance with the embargo date and time (UTC).
2. You specify what we can and cannot share publicly during the embargo.
3. The embargo lifts within 30 days — we won't hold indefinitely.

Breaking an embargo we agreed to invalidates future embargos with that outlet.

## What we will not do

- Pay for coverage. Direct or indirect (affiliate link "reviews", etc.).
- Remove bad reviews. If a reviewer ships something factually wrong, we'll ask for a correction. Opinions stay.
- Grant exclusivity. Every major outlet gets the same treatment.
- Send physical swag. We don't have any. If this changes, it'll be a limited run for launch partners only and announced separately.
